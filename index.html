IRL-Net: Inpainted Region Localization Network via Spatial Attetion

Amir Etefaghi Daryani*, Mahdieh Mirmahdi*, Ahmad Hassanpour*, Hatef Otroshi Shahreza, Bian Yang
To be Submitted, Jun 2023

E2F-GAN:Eyes-to-Face Inpainting via Edge-aware Coarse-to-Fine GANs

Ahmad Hassanpour*, Amir Etefaghi Daryani*, Mahdieh Mirmahdi*, Kiran Raja, Bian Yang, Christoph Busch, and Julian Fierrez
published in IEEE Access, March 2022

This paper aims to reconstruct the face based on the periocular region using two GAN-based modules and an edge predictor.
